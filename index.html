Shawn & Taylor

On a Saturday evening in January 2017, two OKCupid matches met up at an Irish pub in the heart of downtown South Bend.

Shortly after meeting and getting to know each other, we started dating. Many adventures soon followed, including several bike rides and hiking treks, road trips to breweries and wineries, two trips to Lambeau Field, and 36+ hours of travel time to Japan. Within the past years we've become each others' best friends, true loves, and badass partners in life and we look forward to more adventures together.
